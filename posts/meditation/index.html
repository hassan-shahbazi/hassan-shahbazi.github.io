On Meditation Leave - Until 2022 Jan 1st

October 27, 2021   by Hassan Shahbazi

In my culture, or to be more precise, in Islamic culture, there is an approach to meditate and “clean” yourself by doing (or repeating) a behaviour for 40 days. It is called 40ish method, or what we call it in Persian, Chelle Neshini. This is not only in Islam, but in many other religious there is a same sort of instruction. Although I’m not a religious man and this doesn’t aim to improve my relation with God, I think I would like to give it a try. Particularly, due to difficult days I’m experiencing since summer and I have already talked about them in another post.

Thus, I would be away from all social media for 40 days, or even more. Probably until the new year. And by social media, I mean twitter, facebook, instagram, linkedin, and telegram. If you think you need to talk with me, I will still accessible through my email (mailto:hassan@shahbazi.fi) or WhatsApp if you have number. I will either get back as a healthy man, or won’t return anymore.

See you!

Hassan Shahbazi, Oct 2021
